Composition № 26
The Loaded Dot
A planet
a body, held on an orbit
You are here
a place, fixed by a frame
Reduce to the meaningful
A full stop
an end, set in a line of type
MIX · Reduction × Meaning-from-context
One identical dot, three readings. The mark is neutral; the sparse context around it decides whether it is a world, a place, or an ending.
