Composition № 28
Loud / Quiet

LOUD

quietSet small and even, body type asks not to be seen as form at all. It holds one grey colour and one rhythm, line after line, so the eye slides through and the letters dissolve into texture — the page read as a single calm field, nothing reaching up to be looked at.
Juxtaposition · Type-as-image ✗ Type-that-recedes
One word scaled past reading into pure shout; the other dissolved into a grey murmur. Type's two opposite duties — to perform, or to disappear — set against each other in one frame.
