# Prism — ATELIER, the Colour-Plate Portfolio

**Identity (one line):** Couture-constructivism — the Bauhaus trinity (wedge · disc · slab) in a saturated eight-hue mix, shapes crossing and *multiplying into new hues* on a bright ivory field, content riding small geometric plates that hover on the colour; **luxury via impeccable execution of exuberant colour, never by dulling it.**

**Register:** confident · exuberant · precise · expensive · playful-yet-impeccable. Riotous colour held in a couturier's exact hand — the tension IS the brand.

**Purged conceits (do not reintroduce):** no drawn prism, no rainbow/spectrum ordering, no wavelength rails or optical-instrument labels, no coloured header chips. The concept is an abstract geometric colour composition, not "light through a prism."

> **This is a baseline, not the design.** A template sets the starting register, palette, and move-set; the actual design is expected to diverge from it wherever the user's brief, content, or audience demands. See "Baselines, not cages" in SKILL.md.

## Pull it when

- A toolkit, design system, or multi-capability showcase — the *range* is the spectacle
- Data, analytics, or research that should feel vivid and stay rigorous
- A launch or landing that must signal breadth and confidence at a glance
- Anything meant to read alive and unmistakable, backed by evident discipline

## Wrong choice for

- Quiet corporate calm and restraint — that's `meridian.md`
- A dark, cinematic, near-monochrome mood — that's `deepfield.md`
- Hushed heritage luxury — that's `ledger.md` (Prism's quiet sibling: two different maisons — Prism is loud, full-saturated, cool-gunmetal; Ledger is hushed, warm-brass, engraved)
- A single grave note to strike — the full field over-dresses it

## The vocabulary — colour

| Token | Value | Role |
|---|---|---|
| `--ivory` | `#f2eee4` | sheet mat ground (bright, light base) |
| `--plate` | `#fdfcf8` | reading plate (reserved for the densest reading) |
| `--ink` | `#191921` | text ink |
| `--hair` | `#b3b7bf` | THE one cool-graphite hairline keyline weight |
| `--gun` | `#2b333d` | the ONE solid metal fill — the pop, spent once |
| eight field hues | `#ff4a3d` `#ff9415` `#ffd029` `#5ed06b` `#13b6c6` `#2f7dff` `#7a4dff` `#ff43ab` | the saturated mix — alternating FREQUENTLY, never spectrum-ordered |
| deep wells | e.g. `#3a2578` `#14407e` `#0f4e5a` `#125437` | light-ink reading surfaces (all ≥8.6:1) |

Colour is dominant (~60% of the surface); plates float *in* the field, they don't wall it off.

## The vocabulary — type

- **Display:** Fraunces · **Accent italic:** Instrument Serif · **Chrome:** Space Grotesk · **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans · **Figures:** IBM Plex Mono (mid-dot tabular numerals)

## The vocabulary — structure (the fixed move-set)

- **One hairline weight** everywhere; **foil folio numbering**; **museum-grade matting** on plates; tombstone captions
- **Multiply-born hues** — overlapping shapes create new colours (capped multiply so the field stays bright)
- **Geometric cut plates** — clip-path parallelograms / slabs / trapezoids on the 30/60 + vertical grid, never plain rectangles; carrying a colour mix (ink-on-light-colour, light-ink-in-deep-wells, ivory for the densest reading); layered couture drop-shadows so they hover
- Text never sits on a raw multiply crossing — always on a plate or verified pairing
- **Mark:** the abstract Trinity Seal (no prism)
- Fixed plate rhythm; non-spectrum permutation as a held constant

## Held constants (the core — what must survive for it to still be Prism/ATELIER)

The dominant saturated geometric colour-mix (frequent alternation, never spectrum-ordered) · multiply-born hues · ONE hairline keyline weight · geometric cut plates hovering in the field · the couture finish (matting, folio, one cool metal). The specific hue set, plate shapes, and faces are swappable within the floors — the *bright base* and the mix are not.

## Discipline

- One cool gunmetal pop spent once — the exuberance budget is the field, not the accents.
- All four floors hold (AA via verified pairings for every plate fill, chart honesty, legibility, focus/44px). The look bends to the rule, always.
