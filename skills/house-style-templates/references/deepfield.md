# Deepfield — The Deep-Field Notation Plate

**Identity (one line):** A deep-sky observatory notation plate — a dark, year-2200 *field notation* where content is plotted, not marketed: type + plotted points on a warm void, **no website chrome**. You should feel you are standing at a large instrument at night, reading a faint, exact signal.

**Register:** EXPRESSIVE BUT COLD AND DISCIPLINED — the notation is the point, but it never shouts. Mood: cold · vast · precise · quiet, with **one warm human spark against the cold**. It signals rigor and restraint — a look for people who trust measured things.

> **This is a baseline, not the design.** A template sets the starting register, palette, and move-set; the actual design is expected to diverge from it wherever the user's brief, content, or audience demands. See "Baselines, not cages" in SKILL.md.

## Pull it when

- Data, science, and instrument products — telemetry, observatory-grade tools
- Dashboards that must feel *measured*, not busy or salesy
- Editorial data pieces and research tools where rigor is the message
- Anything signalling **precision and restraint**

## Wrong choice for

- Warm, friendly consumer brands — the cold reads as distant
- Playful or expressive products — the discipline reads as severe (→ `terminal.md` or `prism.md`)
- Dense transactional UIs needing warmth and speed over atmosphere
- Anything needing photographic richness or easy approachability

## The vocabulary — colour

| Token | Value | Role |
|---|---|---|
| `--g` | `#141217` | warm-void ground — engineered figure-ground, never "empty space" |
| `--bone` | `#e9e6dd` | bone ink — the readable subject on the void |
| `--soft` | `#938c9b` | soft grey notation |
| `--sig` | `#ff3d7a` | MAGENTA — the single systematic accent; marks every true signal point |
| `--gold` (`--pop`) | `#e6a93a` | the ONE warm pop, spent exactly once, on the single most load-bearing reading |
| `--lat` / `--ln` | `#241f2d` / `#3a3445` | lattice · lines/frames |

Never a second general accent; never gold as broad annotation.

## The vocabulary — type

- **Display:** Bricolage Grotesque (instrument titles)
- **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans (readable field notes)
- **Notation:** IBM Plex Mono — labels, index, data, glyphs; tabular figures on every number
- Three faces, no more; mono is the instrument voice and carries most labels and data.

## The vocabulary — structure (the fixed move-set)

- **Registration-framed plates** — border + four corner ticks + a mono catalog index (`PLATE nn · NAME · roman`)
- **Continuous left declination rail** binding all plates into one instrument sheet
- **Coordinate lattice** (faint radial + rectilinear grid the field is plotted against)
- **Registration rings**, **tick-combs**, **catalog glyphs**
- Imagery is notation- and illustration-led: lattices, rings, plotted points — **drawn, not shot**; duotone-cold if toned at all. Never warm stock photography, lens flare, or a hero image behind a headline.
- **Mark:** the circled-plus pole sigil, magenta stroke — also the favicon. Never recoloured, never reset in a system font.

## Held constants (the core — what must survive for it to still be Deepfield)

The warm-void ground · content *plotted, not marketed* (notation, no website chrome) · the single magenta systematic signal · registration framing (frames, rail, lattice). Everything else — plate count, the gold pop's placement, specific glyphs, faces — is swappable within the floors.

## Discipline

- One magenta systematic signal + one gold pop spent once — that's the whole accent budget.
- All four floors hold (AA measured and published on-plate, chart honesty, legibility, focus/44px). The look bends to the rule, always.
